1) Service Models
- IaaS: Infrastructure as a Service (VMs, storage, networks)
- PaaS: Platform as a Service (managed runtime, DB, frameworks)
- SaaS: Complete applications delivered over the internet

2) AWS Core
- EC2: virtual servers
- S3: object storage (buckets, versioning, lifecycle)
- RDS: managed relational databases
- VPC: isolated virtual network
- IAM: users, roles, policies

3) Best Practices
- Use IAM roles over access keys
- Enable CloudWatch alarms and logging
- Apply least privilege policies
- Use multi-AZ for high availability
